## Immediate Zone (0–5 ft) – *Required*
- [ ] Create and maintain a 0–5 ft non‑combustible buffer around all structures and attachments.
- [ ] Move woodpiles and other combustibles outside 0–5 ft.

## Openings & Vents – *Required*
- [ ] Screen all vents with 1/8‑inch metal mesh.
