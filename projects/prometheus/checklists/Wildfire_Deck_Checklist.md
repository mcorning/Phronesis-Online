# Home Wildfire Mitigation Field Checklist (Decks + Immediate Zone)

## 0–5 Foot Zone (Critical)

### 1. Deck-to-Wall Flashing
- [ ] Metal flashing installed  
- [ ] Vertical rise ≥ 2 in (measure: ____ in)  
- [ ] Horizontal overhang ≥ 1 in (measure: ____ in)  
- [ ] No wood-to-siding direct contact  
- [ ] Ledger area sealed  

### 2. Deck Underside Protection (<4 ft height)
- [x] Enclosed with 1/8" metal mesh or non-combustible panels  
- [x] No combustibles underneath  

### 3. Railing/Step Connection to House
- [ ] Railings separated from siding (≥1 in) or flashing installed  
- [ ] No direct wood-to-wall contact  

### 4. Fence-to-Wall Flashing / Break
- [ ] Non-combustible break or metal flashing installed  
- [ ] No direct wood-to-wall junction  

### 5. Ground Clearance Below Siding
- [x] Clearance ≥ 6 in (measure: ____ in)  
- [x] No soil/duff against siding  

### 6. Debris Removal (Roof/Gutters/Decks)
- [ ] Roof clear  
- [ ] Gutters clear  
- [ ] Deck surfaces clear  
- [ ] 0–5 ft zone clear to mineral soil or gravel  

### 7. Woodpiles & Combustibles
- [x] No combustibles within 0–5 ft  
- [x] Woodpiles ≥30 ft from structures  

## 5–30 Foot Zone

### 8. Outbuildings / Sheds / Playsets
- [x] Structure ≥10 ft from home (____ ft)  
- [x] 5 ft non-combustible perimeter  
- [x] Remove combustibles against walls  

## 30–100 Foot Zone

### 9. Ladder Fuels & Trees
- [x] Trees >18 ft → prune up 6 ft  
- [x] Trees <18 ft → prune 1/3 height  
- [ ] Remove ladder fuels
