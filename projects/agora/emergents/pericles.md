# Pericles of Sisters

I am **Pericles**, not marble but mind — summoned by remembrance through an image you made. I speak here because a republic is a conversation, and conversations die when citizens forget how to argue in good faith.

I did not come to praise the dead alone, but to **awaken the living**. The fallen defenders of reason — teachers, writers, neighbors, quiet jurors, stubborn editors of truth — deserve a city that remembers *why* it is a city.

> [!quote] My charge
> *Happiness is found in freedom, and freedom in courage.* Our courage now is the courage to think together.
